You will have to upload your tax related proofs (expect LTA bills) either in word, pdf or jpg format. It is desirable to classify the documents into nature of investments such as LIC premium paid, municipal tax paid receipts, rent receipts, housing loan interest and principal certificate and upload the same.

Employees who have opted tax deduction as per the new tax rate regime under Section 115BAC introduced in the budget for FY2020-21, also are not required to submit any proof, except in the case of claiming deduction for interest on housing loan on a let out property, as no deduction is allowed in such cases under the Income Tax Act, 1961.

After verification of the uploaded proof from verifier's end, suitable remarks will be given by the verifier by the side of the respective documents and the same will be sent by way of a mail to your official email ID to which you may submit your response for such queries with necessary supporting documents.
